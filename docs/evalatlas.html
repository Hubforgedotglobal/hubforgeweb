About EvalAtlas

EvalAtlas is a global, open-access directory designed to connect the evaluation profession. It serves as a comprehensive platform where evaluators, agencies, vendors, and training institutions can showcase their expertise, connect with opportunities, and strengthen the global evidence ecosystem.

This living atlas makes expertise visible, searchable, and accessible—bridging the gap between local talent and global demand while ensuring that evaluation capacity is recognized and utilized wherever it exists.

At a Glance

  • Type: Open-access platform
  • Scope: Global coverage
  • Access: Free for all users
  • Mission: Connect expertise with opportunity

How EvalAtlas Works

01

Global Mapping

Maps evaluators, evaluation agencies, vendors, and training institutions from around the world in one centralized, searchable database.

02

Expertise Organization

Organizes profiles by skills, methodologies, sectors, geographic focus, languages, and availability to facilitate precise matching.

03

Smart Search

Advanced search and filtering capabilities allow users to quickly find the right expertise for their specific evaluation needs.

04

Local-Global Bridge

Connects local evaluation talent with global opportunities, ensuring diverse perspectives and context-appropriate expertise.

05

Opportunity Visibility

Makes local expertise visible to international organizations seeking culturally competent, locally-rooted evaluators.

06

Community Building

Creates a global community of evaluation professionals who can learn from each other and collaborate across borders.

Who Benefits from EvalAtlas

Individual Evaluators

Showcase your expertise, methodologies, and experience to potential clients and collaborators worldwide. Increase your visibility and access opportunities that match your skills.

Evaluation Agencies

Build your agency profile, highlight your team's capabilities, and connect with organizations seeking evaluation services across different sectors and regions.

Training Institutions

Promote your evaluation training programs, courses, and certifications to a global audience of professionals seeking to build their MERL capacities.

Organizations Seeking Evaluators

Find qualified, culturally competent evaluators who understand your context, speak your language, and bring the right methodological expertise to your projects.

Donors & Funders

Identify evaluation expertise for due diligence, program assessments, and impact studies while supporting diverse, local evaluation capacity.

The Evaluation Community

Strengthen the global evaluation profession by making expertise visible, fostering collaboration, and ensuring quality evaluation capacity is accessible to all.
