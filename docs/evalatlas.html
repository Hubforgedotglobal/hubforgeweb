EvalAtlas

The global directory for the evaluation profession

Making evaluation expertise accessible worldwide

Think LinkedIn meets a global directory—but specifically for the evaluation world. EvalAtlas is an open-access platform where evaluators, MERL practitioners, researchers, and consultants can create profiles, showcase their expertise, and connect with others in the field.

No paywalls. No gatekeeping. Just a simple, searchable directory designed to make the evaluation profession more accessible and connected worldwide.

What Makes EvalAtlas Different

Global & Inclusive

Designed to highlight talent from everywhere—especially practitioners who are underrepresented in global evaluation spaces.

Free & Open Access

No membership fee, no subscription, no premium tiers. Everyone gets the same access.

Searchable & Smart

Filter by expertise area, geography, language, sector, methodology—whatever matters for your project.

Community-Driven

Practitioners control their own profiles. You decide what to share and how people can reach you.

Who Should Use EvalAtlas

Evaluators & MERL Practitioners

Build your professional profile, showcase your expertise, and get discovered by organizations looking for someone with your skills.

Organizations Looking for Talent

Find the right expertise without relying solely on personal networks or expensive consulting firms.

Students & Emerging Evaluators

Connect with mentors, find opportunities, and start building your professional network.

Evaluation Networks & Associations

Promote your members and make it easier for people to discover the expertise within your network.
