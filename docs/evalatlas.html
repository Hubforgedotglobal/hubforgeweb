EvalAtlas

The global directory for the evaluation profession

What is EvalAtlas?

Think LinkedIn meets a global directory—but specifically for the evaluation world. EvalAtlas is an open-access platform where evaluators, MERL practitioners, researchers, and consultants can create profiles, showcase their expertise, and connect with others in the field.

Whether you're an organization looking for the right evaluator for your project, a student trying to find a mentor, or a practitioner wanting to expand your professional network—EvalAtlas makes it easier to find the people you need.

No paywalls. No gatekeeping. Just a simple, searchable directory designed to make the evaluation profession more accessible and connected worldwide.

What Makes EvalAtlas Different

Global & Inclusive

We're not just focused on evaluators from big consulting firms or well-known universities. EvalAtlas is designed to highlight talent from everywhere—especially practitioners who are underrepresented in global evaluation spaces.

Free & Open Access

There's no membership fee, no subscription, no premium tiers. Everyone gets the same access. We believe evaluation expertise should be accessible to anyone who needs it, regardless of their budget.

Searchable & Smart

Filter by expertise area, geography, language, sector, methodology—whatever matters for your project. Find exactly the kind of evaluator you're looking for without scrolling through endless profiles.

Community-Driven

Practitioners control their own profiles. You decide what to share, how to present your expertise, and how people can reach you. No algorithmic mystery—just straightforward networking.

Who Should Use EvalAtlas?

Evaluators & MERL Practitioners

Build your professional profile, showcase your expertise, get discovered by organizations looking for someone with your skills, and connect with peers across the globe.

Organizations Looking for Talent

Need to hire an evaluator for a project? Looking for pro bono support? EvalAtlas helps you find the right expertise without relying solely on personal networks or expensive consulting firms.

Students & Emerging Evaluators

Connect with mentors, find opportunities, learn from experienced practitioners, and start building your professional network before you even graduate.

Evaluation Networks & Associations

Promote your members, increase visibility for your community, and make it easier for people to discover the expertise within your network.

How to Get Started

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up and build your profile. Share your expertise, experience, sectors you work in, methodologies you use, languages you speak—whatever helps people understand what you bring to the table.

2

Browse & Connect

Use filters to search for evaluators by location, expertise, sector, or methodology. Found someone doing interesting work? Reach out directly through their preferred contact method.

3

Keep It Updated

As you gain new skills, complete new projects, or shift your focus areas, update your profile. The more current it is, the easier it is for the right opportunities to find you.

Join the EvalAtlas community

Whether you're building your network, looking for expertise, or simply want to be part of a global evaluation directory—EvalAtlas is here for you.
