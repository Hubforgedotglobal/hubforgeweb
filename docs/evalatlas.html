EvalAtlas

The Global Directory for the Evaluation Profession

Global Directory Open Access Expert Matching

What is EvalAtlas?

Think LinkedIn meets a global directory—but specifically for the evaluation world. EvalAtlas is where evaluators, evaluation firms, training institutions, and anyone involved in measuring social impact can create a profile, showcase their work, and get discovered.

The problem we're solving? It's too hard to find the right evaluator. Organizations searching for evaluation expertise often default to big-name consultants or people in their immediate network. Meanwhile, talented local evaluators—the ones who actually understand the context—remain invisible.

EvalAtlas changes that. It's a searchable, open-access platform that makes expertise visible no matter where you are in the world. Need an evaluator who speaks Swahili and specializes in education programs in East Africa? You can find them. Looking for an agency with experience in participatory methods? They're here too.

At a Glance

  • Type: Open-access platform
  • Scope: Global coverage
  • Access: Free for all users
  • Mission: Connect expertise with opportunity

How EvalAtlas Works

01

One Central Directory

Instead of Googling endlessly or asking for recommendations in Facebook groups, you get one searchable database of evaluators, agencies, vendors, and training programs from around the world.

02

Find Exactly Who You Need

Search by skills, methodologies, sectors, geographic focus, languages spoken, and availability. No more settling for "close enough"—find the perfect match for your evaluation needs.

03

Smart Filters

Advanced search lets you narrow down results fast. Filter by budget range, years of experience, specific certifications, or evaluation approaches to get exactly what you're looking for.

04

Connect Local & Global

International organizations find local expertise. Local evaluators access global opportunities. Everyone benefits when talent and need can actually find each other.

05

Level the Playing Field

You don't need fancy marketing or a big brand to be found. If you do good work and create a profile, organizations looking for your exact expertise will be able to discover you.

06

Build Community

Connect with fellow evaluators, learn from peers worldwide, and collaborate across borders. Evaluation doesn't have to be a solo profession.

Who Benefits from EvalAtlas

Individual Evaluators

Stop relying solely on word-of-mouth referrals. Build your profile once and let organizations discover you based on your actual expertise, not just who you know.

Evaluation Agencies

Showcase your team's capabilities and past work in one place. When organizations search for evaluation services in your specialty, they'll find you—not just the big-name firms with huge marketing budgets.

Training Institutions

Promote your evaluation training programs, courses, and certifications to a global audience of professionals seeking to build their MERL capacities.

Organizations Hiring Evaluators

Stop settling for whoever responds to your RFP first. Search for evaluators who actually understand your context, speak the local language, and have the exact methodological expertise you need.

Donors & Funders

Identify evaluation expertise for due diligence, program assessments, and impact studies while supporting diverse, local evaluation capacity.

The Evaluation Community

Strengthen the global evaluation profession by making expertise visible, fostering collaboration, and ensuring quality evaluation capacity is accessible to all.
