About the Impact Lab

The HubForge Impact Lab is our flagship initiative designed to strengthen the Monitoring, Evaluation, Research, and Learning (MERL) ecosystem at the grassroots level. We provide volunteer-driven support to nonprofits and community-based organisations that often lack access to specialized MERL expertise.

By equipping these organisations with essential MERL skills, tools, and resources, the Impact Lab empowers them to generate data-driven insights, improve decision-making, and enhance program effectiveness.

Quick Facts

  • Focus: Grassroots organizations
  • Model: Volunteer-driven support
  • Approach: Hands-on capacity building
  • Impact: Evidence-based decision making

What the Impact Lab Offers

01

MERL Framework Design

Help organizations develop robust monitoring and evaluation frameworks tailored to their unique context, including theories of change, logic models, and indicator frameworks.

02

Data Collection Support

Provide hands-on assistance in designing data collection tools, implementing surveys, conducting interviews, and facilitating focus group discussions.

03

Capacity Building Workshops

Deliver practical training sessions on MERL fundamentals, data analysis techniques, visualization methods, and evidence-based reporting.

04

Tools & Resources

Share templates, guides, and open-source tools that make MERL more accessible and less resource-intensive for grassroots organizations.

05

Learning Community

Connect organizations with peers facing similar challenges, creating a supportive network for shared learning and collaboration.

06

Ongoing Mentorship

Pair organizations with experienced MERL practitioners who provide ongoing guidance, troubleshooting, and technical support.

Who We Support

Grassroots Nonprofits

Small and medium-sized organizations working at the community level who need MERL expertise but lack the budget for external consultants.

Community-Based Organizations

Local groups led by community members who are closest to the challenges they're addressing and need tools to measure their impact.

Youth-Led Initiatives

Young changemakers launching innovative programs who want to build evidence-based practices from the start of their journey.

Social Enterprises

Mission-driven businesses that need to track both social impact and financial sustainability through rigorous measurement.

How the Impact Lab Works

1

Application & Matching

Organizations apply through our simple online form. We review applications and match you with volunteer MERL practitioners based on your specific needs and context.

2

Needs Assessment

We conduct an initial consultation to understand your current MERL practices, challenges, and goals. Together, we develop a tailored support plan.

3

Hands-On Support

Our volunteer experts work alongside your team, providing practical assistance in designing frameworks, collecting data, and building internal capacity.

4

Knowledge Transfer

Throughout the engagement, we focus on building your team's skills so you can sustain MERL practices independently after the support period ends.

5

Ongoing Community

You join our alumni network, gaining access to resources, peer learning opportunities, and continued support from the HubForge community.

Ready to strengthen your MERL capacity?

Whether you're just starting with monitoring and evaluation or looking to strengthen existing practices, the Impact Lab can help. Our volunteer-driven model means high-quality support at no cost to your organization.
