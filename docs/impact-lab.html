HubForge Impact Lab

Strengthening the MERL Ecosystem at the Grassroots

MERL Training Volunteer-Driven Grassroots Focus

What is the Impact Lab?

Here's the reality: Most grassroots organizations don't have a MERL expert on staff. They're doing incredible work in their communities, but when it comes to measuring impact, designing surveys, or building evaluation frameworks—they're often on their own.

That's where the Impact Lab comes in. We connect these organizations with skilled volunteers who bring real MERL expertise. Think of it as pro bono consulting meets hands-on mentorship. Our volunteers work alongside local teams, helping them build the monitoring and evaluation systems they need to understand what's working, what's not, and why it matters.

The goal isn't just to do the work for them—it's to build lasting capacity so they can continue measuring their impact long after our support ends.

Quick Facts

  • Focus: Grassroots organizations
  • Model: Volunteer-driven support
  • Approach: Hands-on capacity building
  • Impact: Evidence-based decision making

What the Impact Lab Offers

01

Build Your MERL Framework

We help you design a monitoring and evaluation system that actually makes sense for your work. Whether it's building a theory of change, creating a logic model, or choosing the right indicators—we make it practical, not theoretical.

02

Design Better Data Collection

Need to run a survey? Conduct interviews? Facilitate focus groups? We work with you to create data collection tools that get you the insights you need without overwhelming your team or community.

03

Hands-On Training

No boring PowerPoints here. We deliver practical, interactive workshops that teach your team how to analyze data, visualize results, and tell compelling stories with evidence.

04

Free Templates & Tools

Why reinvent the wheel? We share ready-to-use templates, guides, and open-source tools that save you time and money while helping you do better evaluation work.

05

Join a Learning Community

You're not alone in this. We connect you with other organizations facing similar challenges so you can learn from each other, share what works, and build together.

06

Get Ongoing Mentorship

Stuck on something? Our experienced MERL practitioners are here for you—providing ongoing guidance, answering questions, and helping you troubleshoot challenges as they come up.

Who We Support

Grassroots Nonprofits

You're doing great work, but hiring a MERL consultant isn't in the budget. We get it. That's exactly who we're here for—small and mid-sized organizations that need expertise without the price tag.

Community-Based Organizations

No one knows your community better than you do. We help you build the measurement systems to prove what you already know is working—and discover what could work even better.

Youth-Led Initiatives

Starting something new? Building evidence-based practices from day one is way easier than trying to retrofit them later. We'll help you set up the right systems now so you can focus on making impact.

Social Enterprises

Balancing mission and money is hard enough. We help you track both your social impact and financial sustainability so you can show investors and communities that you're delivering on both fronts.

How the Impact Lab Works

1

Apply & Get Matched

Fill out a quick application telling us about your organization and what you need help with. We'll pair you with a volunteer who has the right skills and understands your context.

2

Understand Your Needs

We start with a conversation—what are you currently doing for M&E? What's working? What's frustrating? Where do you want to go? Then we build a plan together.

3

Work Side by Side

This isn't about us doing the work for you. We work with your team—building frameworks, designing tools, analyzing data—so you learn while you're doing it.

4

Build Your Capacity

Our goal is to make ourselves unnecessary. By the time we're done, your team should feel confident running your own MERL systems without needing outside help.

5

Stay Connected

Once the formal support ends, you're still part of the HubForge family. Join our alumni network for ongoing resources, peer connections, and community support whenever you need it.

Let's build your MERL capacity together

Whether you're starting from scratch or trying to level up what you're already doing, we're here to help. The best part? It's completely free—all we ask is that you're ready to learn and committed to using evidence to drive your impact.
