What is this?

A hands-on program exploring one question: How do decisions actually get made, and how could they be better?

You'll work on real problems. Test ideas. Build things. Take ownership. This is not a traditional internship with predefined tasks.

Four Roles

Research & Insights

Turn messy information into clear insight.

Design & Strategy

Translate complex ideas into compelling stories.

Program Management

Turn ideas into action. Keep things moving.

AI & Technology

Build tools that help people decide better.

Who should apply?

Early-career people who want depth over exposure. Comfortable with ambiguity. Curious about how evidence and judgment come together. Ready to build, not just observe.
