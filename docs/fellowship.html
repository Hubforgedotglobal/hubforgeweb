The Fellowship

Foundations for better decisions

What is this?

A learning-by-building program exploring one question: How do decisions actually get made, and how could they be better?

The fellowship focuses on the thinking, framing, and systems behind decisions in technology, research, programs, and organizations.

What you'll do

→

Work on real, unresolved problems

→

Test ideas, refine them, sometimes drop them

→

Think critically, ask questions, take ownership

This is not a traditional internship with predefined tasks.

Roles

Apply to one or two based on how you think and work.

Research & Insights

Understand contexts, users, and decision environments. Turn ambiguity into insight.

Design & Strategy

Shape narratives, design clear content, and build a thoughtful public voice.

Program Management

Coordinate people and systems. Turn intent into execution.

AI & Technology

Design and experiment with tools that support human judgment.

Who should apply?

✓

Early in your career and want depth, not just exposure

✓

Comfortable working with ambiguity

✓

Care about how evidence, insight, and judgment come together

✓

Want skills that stay useful across roles and sectors

✓

Entrepreneur mindset

Why does this exist?

Better decisions don't come from better intentions alone. They come from clearer thinking, stronger foundations, and respect for complexity.

This fellowship helps people build those foundations early.
