The Fellowship

Foundations for better decisions

How do decisions actually get made?

The HubForge × EvalAtlas Fellowship is a hands-on opportunity to work on one core question: How do decisions actually get made — and how could they be better?

This fellowship focuses on the thinking, framing, and systems that sit behind decisions across technology, research, programs, and organisations. You'll work on real, unresolved problems — testing ideas, refining them, and sometimes dropping them.

This is not a traditional internship with predefined tasks. You'll be expected to think, ask questions, and take ownership.

Roles You Can Apply For

Apply to one or two roles based on how you like to think and work. All roles are equally important.

Research & Insights

Understand contexts, users, and decision environments. Turn ambiguity into insight. Perfect for those who love digging deep and finding patterns others miss.

Design & Strategy

Shape narratives, design clear content, and build a thoughtful public voice. Ideal for those who can translate complex ideas into compelling stories.

Program Management

Coordinate people and systems. Turn intent into execution. For those who thrive on making things happen and keeping projects moving forward.

AI & Technology

Design and experiment with tools that support human judgment. Build systems that help people make better decisions, not replace them.

Who Should Apply

Early Career Seekers

You're early in your career and want depth, not just exposure. You're looking for skills that stay useful across roles and sectors.

Comfortable with Ambiguity

You don't need every answer handed to you. You enjoy exploring problems that don't have obvious solutions.

Evidence-Curious

You care about how evidence, insight, and judgment come together. You want to understand how good decisions happen.

Entrepreneur Mindset

You take initiative. You see opportunities where others see obstacles. You're ready to build, not just observe.

Practical Details

01

Duration

8–12 weeks, with 6–10 hours per week. Flexible enough to fit alongside other commitments.

02

Format

Fully remote. Small cohort for focused learning. You'll work closely with a dedicated team.

03

Compensation

This is an unpaid, voluntary fellowship. The value is in the learning, the network, and the experience of building something real.

04

Selection

Based on curiosity, clarity of thought, and willingness to engage deeply. No specific degree or background required.

Why does this fellowship exist?

Better decisions don't come from better intentions alone. They come from clearer thinking, stronger foundations, and respect for complexity.

This fellowship exists to help people build those foundations early — before habits form, before assumptions harden, before the pressure of "getting it right" crowds out the space for genuine learning.

Is This Right for You?

This fellowship is a good fit if you enjoy:

Thinking deeply about problems

Learning by doing

Working on problems still taking shape

Building with an entrepreneur mindset
