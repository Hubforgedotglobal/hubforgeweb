Glocal Impact Awards

Celebrating grassroots evidence champions

Recognition for those doing the real work

When people talk about "global leaders in evaluation," they usually mean consultants from well-known firms or academics at prestigious universities. Meanwhile, there are brilliant evaluators, grassroots organizers, and community leaders doing exceptional MERL work—but nobody outside their immediate circle knows their names.

The Glocal Impact Awards for M&E celebrate grassroots evidence champions—the ones who might not have fancy degrees or big budgets, but who are proving every day that great evaluation work happens everywhere.

Award Categories

Individual Champion

For the person championing evidence-based practices in their community—building M&E systems, training others, and showing what's possible even when resources are tight.

Grassroots Organization

For community organizations that have made M&E a core part of how they work—not because a donor demanded it, but because they genuinely want to learn and improve.

Youth Innovator

For young changemakers (under 35) doing things differently—bringing fresh ideas to M&E, using creative approaches, and inspiring their peers to care about evidence.

Collaborative Impact

For partnerships or networks that prove collaboration works—pooling resources, sharing knowledge, and building evaluation capacity together.

Why Recognition Matters

Spotlight the Unsung Heroes

There are people doing incredible M&E work who deserve recognition because their stories can inspire others and show what's possible.

Show What's Possible

When you see someone like you doing amazing evaluation work in a similar context, it suddenly feels achievable.

Connect Different Worlds

Global evaluation networks need to hear from grassroots practitioners. These awards help bridge that gap.

Make MERL More Valued

Celebrating great M&E work sends a message: this stuff matters. It encourages more organizations to prioritize evidence.
